MISSION


(why we exist)

Creators’ Thrift is a Chicago artists resource center working to lay down the groundwork for artists to leave institutional support. We are a BIPOC and LBGTQIA+ centered organization that builds pathways for artist independence and sovereignty while creating a growing network accessible to all Chicago artists.

VISION


(what we want to do)
  1. Creating an aggressive community outreach that puts the art back in the hands of artists.
  2. Art and community woven together for knowledge sharing and accessible resources.
  3. Art bringing community together for freely shared resources without expecting artists’ labor in return.

STRATEGY


(our game plan)
  1. Ultimately achieve theater institution liquidation and independence from theater institutions.
  2. Survey the art community every quarter to expand resources.
  3. Release a monthly newsletter to give updates on the organization.
  4. Monthly network meeting featuring a NONO artist.
