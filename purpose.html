OUR PURPOSE



MISSION


(why we exist)

Creators Thrift is an artistic community that establishes a mutual aid network to lay down the groundwork for artists to leave institutional support for artistic production and art-making practices. Based in Chicago, we are a group that has been created by and for people who have been intentionally disinvested and ignored by systemic oppression and discrimination. Creators Thrift is an organization that moves in collaboration with our community to build pathways for artistic sovereignty while creating a growing network and resource center accessible to all Chicago artists.

VISION


(what we want to do)
  1. We are a community of abundant growth and infinite creation. We are a community that supports and empowers all artists in all of their magic, intersecting identities, multiple talents, and range of experience.
  2. Craft art and community that is woven together for knowledge sharing and accessible resources.
  3. Hold an artistic community that comes together for freely shared resources without expecting artists’ labor in return.
  4. Ultimately achieve theater/arts institution liquidation and independence from all theater institutions.

STRATEGY


(our game plan)
  1. Establish a mutual aid network for commerce, but NOT capitalism. Members of this mutual aid network will barter with fellow artists to exchange goods, service, and knowledge.
  2. Survey the art community every quarter to expand resources, assess community needs, and continuously work to improve and strengthen the community.
  3. Host public events that further our shared community knowledge and build solidarity among art creators in Chicago
