Kiko (K/Kiko)


Perfect Angel

Kiko (K/Kiko) is an Agender storyteller, producer and graphic designer. Kiko’s hobbies are painting and creating art out of recyclables. K is always excited for the opportunity to learn and grow.

BeeBee Cooper Browne (they/she)


Messenger Hawk

BeeBee Cooper Browne (they/she) is a producer and artistic creator in Chicago whose work explores multimedia protest extravaganzas. They have survived 6 years in the Chicago non-profit theatre arts community as an administrator, playwright, and producer - such experiences have fueled her passion to work to dismantle the oppressive structures in place for art production. As the messenger hawk for Creators' Thrift, BeeBee will connect network members and facilitate resource exchanges. They cannot wait to build stronger artistic and community relationships with you!

Jessi Realzola (she/her)


Artist Freedom Advocate

Jessi Realzola (she/her) is a Chicago based comedic actor, writer, improvisor, and producer. Jessi’s performer credits include video sketch for Love Rules and Pre-Existing web series, various sketch shows, film festivals throughout the year, CSBF dance performer, Chicago Women’s Funny Festival, Femprovisor Fest, Fuacata Festival, Bentwood Festival, Compass Improv Festival, and WinterFest. She has traveled the country to perform in CA, TX, FL, MO, and WI. She performs with her comedy groups Las Tinas, Ratas de Dos Patas, and Heraldo. Jessi uses her rich Mexican background as a source for inspiration in her characters and as material when she’s creating original sketches and web pieces. She has produced variety comedy shows all over Chicago that focus on highlighting diverse and underrepresented talent. Jessi believes that building shows for minority and diverse talent is important for the growth and advancement of art as a whole. As an artist freedom advocate with Creator’s Thrift, Jessi hopes to offer the opportunity for artists to build many bridges in order for them to create amazing things without the limitations of an institution and with the independence to be themselves.
